Regexp: (\W|^)Hello*(\W|$)
subject string: *Hello Hello Hello*
explicatii: \W matches any character thatï¿½s not a letter, digit, or underscore. It prevents the regex from matching letters before or after the word.
^ matches the start of a new line. 
$ matches the end of a line. 
n* matches any string that contains zero or more occurrences of n
