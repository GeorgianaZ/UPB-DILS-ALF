solutia 2
Regexp: (\W|^)Hello*(\W|$)
subject string: *Hello Hello Hello*
explicatii: \W matches any character that’s not a letter, digit, or underscore. It prevents the regex from matching letters before or after the word.
^ matches the start of a new line. 
$ matches the end of a line. 
n* matches any string that contains zero or more occurrences of n

solutia 3
Regexp: h[e!][l@]l[o@]
Subject string: *hello hello hello*
explicatii: 
\W is not included, so that other characters can appear before or after any of the variants of hello. For example, the regex still matches hello in the following text:
hello!! or ***hello***
